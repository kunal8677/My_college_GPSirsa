MECHANICAL ENGG.

Mechanical Engineering, at the diploma level, is a dynamic and rewarding field that provides a strong foundation in the principles and practices of computer systems and technology. This program offers a unique opportunity for individuals to enter the world of technology and engineering with a practical, hands-on approach.

Course Duration - 3 years
Degree Level - Diploma
Semester Fees - 2850/-

1st Semester

Subjects :-

  • English and Communication Skills - I
  • Applied Mathematics - I
  • Applied Physics - I
  • Fundamentals of IT
  • Engineering Graphics
  • Environmental Studies & Disaster Management
  • General Workshop Practice

2nd Semester

Subjects :-

  • Applied Chemistry
  • Applied Mathematics-II
  • Applied Physics - II
  • Applied Mechanics
  • Mechanical Engineering Drawing- I
  • Workshop Technology -I
  • Workshop Practice- I

3rd Semester

Subjects :-

  • Industrial/In-House Training - I
  • Strength of Materials
  • Mechanical Engineering Drawing II
  • Thermodynamics - I
  • Workshop Technology – II
  • Basics of Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Workshop Practice – II
  • Open Elective (MOOCs+ /Offline)

4th Semester

Subjects :-

  • English and Communication Skills - II
  • Materials and Metallurgy
  • Hydraulics and Pneumatics
  • Workshop Technology – III
  • Machine Design
  • Thermodynamics II
  • Workshop Practice – III
  • CAD/CAM

5th Semester

Subjects :-

  • Theory of Machines
  • Refrigeration and Air-conditioning
  • Machine Design
  • CNC Machines and Automation
  • Workshop Technology-III
  • Workshop Practice –III

6th Semester

Subjects :-

  • Automobile Engineering
  • Inspection and Quality Control
  • Estimation and Costing
  • Entrepreneurship Development & Management
  • Elective
  • Project Work

News Letters

Govt. Polytechnic is situated in the west of the Sirsa City. Its distance is about 5 KM from Main Bus stand & about 4 KM from the Railway station of the city on Rania/Ellenabad road. The institution is spread over an area of 26 acres and has a very good & green campus. It came into existence in 1962 at the time of Joint Punjab by the efforts of Sh. Sardar Partap Singh Kairon, the then Chief Minister of the Joint Punjab.
